Pride and Prejudice

What is Pride and Prejudice?

Pride and Prejudice is a renowned novel by Jane Austen, published in 1813, that explores themes of love, marriage, and societal expectations in 19th-century England.

The story revolves around an independent and spirited Elizabeth Bennet, who navigates the challenges of a conservative society with a strong emphasis on class and wealth.
