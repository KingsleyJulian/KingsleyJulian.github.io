Why read classic Literature?

Classic literature offers timeless insights and understanding of stories on the era it was written. By exploring different cultures, historical periods, and universal themes, it provides a broader perspective on life.

Additionally, reading classics enhances literary appreciation, language skills, and fosters introspection in a fast-paced world.

About Great Books

As the name suggests it is a subject that tackles the Greatest of Books, which contained profound lessons on the era they were written.

Pieces of literature that wrote the era we are in, and pieced the childhood of many others and possible even the future generations.
